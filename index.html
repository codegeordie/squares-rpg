Welcome to RPGSquares!

Your health and actionpoints(AP) are in the center pane.
Click the top left to see your stats and level up.
The four squares around you are your options to explore.
Find monsters, treasure, or health.  
Pay attention to the level of the square before you choose it.

The bottom left is your inventory, click on items to move them.
To get rid of an item, move it to the trash in the upper right.

The bottom right are your attacks.  Click them to defeat monsters in turn-based combat.  Weapons and shields grant special attacks, so itemize carefully.

Pay attention to highlighted squares if you get lost!

Good luck!
